### Added

**`house_style_decomposition` — nested-baseline idiolect-vs-house attribution-of-variation (spec-wave-4 Tier-4a, M1; new surface `house_style_decomposition`).** A new surface that runs a target writer's stylometric feature vector against a curated ORDERED LADDER of nested baselines (`same_author_same_org` / `different_context` / `different_authors_same_org` / `same_genre_outside_org` / `broad_reference`) and reports, per feature family, the **per-level Burrows-Delta**, the **signed idiolect-vs-house contrast** (`D[different_authors_same_org] − D[different_context]` with POSITIVE = idiolect-borne), and a **descriptive attribution label** (`idiolect_borne` / `house_borne` / `shared_or_indistinct`) relative to this specific ladder. NEVER a verdict, an authorship call, a probability, or a population band.

The decomposition implements the classic register/idiolect partition of authorship attribution (Biber register variation; **Burrows (2002) "'Delta': a Measure of Stylistic Difference and a Guide to Likely Authorship", *Computers and the Humanities* 37(3)**). The novelty is the nested-baseline ladder + leakage guard, not a new model. M1 reuses `stylometry_core.compare_to_baseline(..., include_spacy=False)` — pure-stdlib, no numpy/spaCy/torch, deterministic, CI-runnable.

**Leakage guard (mechanical, load-time, fail-loud):** entries at `different_authors_same_org` / `same_genre_outside_org` / `broad_reference` must not carry the target `author_id`; the target file's path must not appear in any level. Inline `text` in a manifest entry is **forbidden** — it bypasses the path-identity leakage check (`_exclude_target_path` only catches entries with a `resolved_path`; an inline entry has `resolved_path=None` and passes through unchecked). Every entry must supply `text_path`. Missing `text_path` or presence of inline `text` → `HouseStyleError` → `bad_input` refusal.

**M2 seam (lazy-import, fail-loud, NOT in this build):** `--lens embedding` refuses with `missing_dependency` **whether or not torch is importable** — M1 wires no embedding subspace and the import-SUCCESS branch is explicitly refused (the planted-false-invariant pattern from `distinct_diversity_audit`).

Posture: **no verdict.** No top-level score or band. `shared_or_indistinct` is the designed center, not an error. Attribution is within-this-ladder descriptive only; the claim license refuses authorship, de-anonymization, "real voice" certificates, and any selection/fitness use. Calibration PROVISIONAL (`status: heuristic`; `--margin 0.15` is an operator knob, not a calibrated cut). Ships 17 stdlib tests including the unconditional no-verdict recursive key-walk guard and the inline-text / leakage / acceptance-gate battery.
