### Added

**`lambdag_audit` — the LambdaG grammar likelihood-ratio AV signal (spec 31; `voice_coherence` surface).** A white-box, **model-free** authorship-verification signal: build a count-based n-gram language model over a document's **POS-tag sequences** and score a query document's grammar log-likelihood under a **reference-author** POS-LM versus a **background** POS-LM. The headline is the signed log-likelihood-ratio `lambda_g = logL_ref − logL_bg` (length-normalized `lambda_g_per_token`); `> 0` means the query's grammar is more probable under the reference author's model than under the population's. The likelihood-ratio sibling of `voice_distance.py`'s Burrows Delta — same `voice_coherence` surface, same no-verdict posture — but answered with a generative LM and a log-LR rather than a standardized frequency-space distance. Both halves of the ratio plus a per-sentence λ_G list and the top author-/background-favoring n-grams are surfaced for audit. Rooted in: Nini et al. 2024, "Grammar as a Behavioral Biometric: Using Cognitively Motivated Grammar Models for Authorship Verification" ([arXiv:2403.08462](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.08462)). Implements `specs/31-lambdag.md` (M1).

**M1 is stdlib and CI-runnable without a model.** The n-gram LM is pure `collections` + `math` (a `Counter` over `(context, tag)` with add-k / Lidstone smoothing and `<s>`/`</s>` sentence padding over the closed UPOS vocabulary), tested directly on fixture POS streams with `HAS_SPACY` monkeypatched False. The spaCy POS parse (shared `variance_audit._NLP`, via the new `stylometry_core.pos_tag_sentences` seam that `pos_trigram_features` now also uses — one POS path, no drift) is the **only** model-gated step; with no parser the surface **abstains** (`available:false` / `missing_dependency`) — there is no faithful parse-free POS stream. M2 (deferred) swaps the POS alphabet and/or smoothing behind a lazy import; the schema, band, license, and wiring are frozen here.

**Posture: NO VERDICT (`status: heuristic`).** No `is_ai` / `is_human` / `same_author` / `different_author` / `verdict` / `match` / `prob_same_author` key anywhere (a recursive-walk test pins this at every envelope depth); the only categorical is a 3-level PROVISIONAL **leaning** band (`background_leaning` / `indeterminate` / `author_leaning`, the flat `surprisal_audit._provisional_band` shape + a `calibration_status` string). The surface scores **one** query against **one** named reference/background pair and **never ranks authors** (verification, not attribution — a never-selects test asserts no argmax field). **Anti-Goodhart:** when reference and background are drawn from one manifest the loader asserts the two document sets are **held-out disjoint by id** (a doc in both inflates the LR — train-on-the-test-set); self-scoring a corpus against itself is refused. Corpus selection uses the real `stylometry_core` loaders (`--reference-dir`/`--background-dir` or `--manifest` + the fixed `persona`/`split`/`register` filters, exactly as `voice_distance`) — no invented K=V filter. The claim license refuses the same-author **and** the AI/human inferences in words; thresholds are operator-side / PROVISIONAL (calibration is the validation harness's job). 29 tests (LR-math pins + bounds-gate + posture guards run parser-free; end-to-end cases `skipif(not HAS_SPACY)`).
