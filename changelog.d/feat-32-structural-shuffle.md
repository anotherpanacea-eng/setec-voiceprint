### Added

**`structural_shuffle_audit` — perplexity shift under sentence/word shuffling (spec 32, M1).** A new Surface-5 discrimination-evidence capability (`id: structural_shuffle_audit`, surface `discrimination_structural_shuffle`) that probes how much a text's predictability depends on the *ordering* of its parts. It scores three variants of a window under a single causal LM — the original, a sentence-shuffled variant, and a word-shuffled variant — and reports 5 features describing the perplexity jump (`ppl_sum`, `ppl_diff`, `ppl_ratio`, `ppl_log_ratio`, `ppl_pct_change`). A **larger** jump = a **more order-dependent** text = **more human-like** (the hypothesis direction, pinned by a sign test; silent inversion is the perturbation family's shared failure mode). The signal is a structural, order-dependence axis **orthogonal in mechanism** to the natural-ordering probability family (Binoculars cross-perplexity ratio, Fast-DetectGPT curvature, DivEye surprisal moments) — it scores *permuted* inputs, they score the natural ordering.

Rooted in **Luminol-AIDetect ([arXiv:2604.25860](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25860), 2026) — PROVISIONAL / UNVERIFIED preprint**; M0 paper-confirmation is a hard gate before the M2 model run, and the method falls back to an independent reimplementation in the DetectGPT perturbation family (Mitchell et al. 2023, [arXiv:2301.11305](https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.11305)) if the preprint is unconfirmed. No FPR/AUC number is asserted as fact — the paper's news-domain 0.001 FPR is **not** carried into the claim license; lit-fiction calibration is empirical.

**This ships M1 only: model-free orchestration, CI-testable.** The sentence splitter (spaCy with regex fallback), the seeded sentence/word shuffles, and the 5-feature arithmetic are pure Python; `score_window` accepts injected perplexities so no model loads in CI. The GPT-Neo-2.7B scorer (3 forward passes per window) is the M2 GPU seam, gated on the M0 paper check + CPU-first model verification — **not** built here.

**Posture (no-verdict / anti-Goodhart).** `status: heuristic`; ships with **no threshold and no verdict band** (default `"uncalibrated"`), refuses an is_ai/is_human label, and must never be the held-out SETEC fitness signal. The claim license names the **ESL / non-native-English false-positive failure mode** (non-idiomatic, less order-dependent prose can falsely resemble the AI profile — the GECScore Achilles heel) and the UNVERIFIED preprint status. A static separation guard pins that the script imports nothing from `{fitness, setec_signals, loop, qlora, reviser, cosplay, splits, provenance}` or from `binoculars_audit`. New surface registered drop-in via `claim_license_surfaces/discrimination_structural_shuffle.txt`; capability via a `capabilities.d/` fragment + per-id `_golden_capabilities/` golden (no `==N` count literal). torch-free test suite (stub backend).
