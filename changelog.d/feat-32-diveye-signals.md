### Added

**`diveye_signals.py` — DivEye surprisal-DIVERSITY signal aggregation (spec 32, M1; arXiv:2509.18880).** A stdlib-only, model-free math helper that adds the four DivEye temporal / distribution-shape signals the SETEC arsenal did not already ship — the 1st-order difference (delta) series, the 2nd-order difference (acceleration) series, the Shannon entropy of the surprisal histogram, and the lag-1 ACF of the acceleration series — and `aggregate_diveye_signals(surprisal_series)`, which assembles the full nine-signal DivEye vector by **reusing** `surprisal_audit.py`'s already-tested mean/variance/skew/excess-kurtosis/ACF helpers (lazy import, stdlib fallback) for F1–F4/F9 rather than duplicating the math. Runs over an INJECTED per-token surprisal series in M1 (CI-runnable, no torch/model/GPU); the real surprisal forward pass and the registered `diveye_audit.py` discrimination surface are the experiment-gated M2 seam and are **not** in this build. `TASK_SURFACE = None` — this is a descriptive math helper, not a registered detection surface: it emits no verdict / band / `calibration_status` and couples to none of the fitness / calibration / discrimination / validation / loop modules. The signed moments (skew, excess kurtosis, lag-1 ACFs) are emitted under un-prefixed keys (`skew`, `excess_kurtosis`, `acf1`, `accel_acf1`) so the aggregate passes `output_schema.validate_results_bounds` for any finite input including negative values — the REVIEW's CRITICAL "output_schema range-check trap" (`_SURPRISAL_RE` enforces `>= 0` on any `surprisal`/`entropy`-token key), pinned by a test. `min_acf_length=30` matches `surprisal_audit.MIN_SERIES_FOR_ACF`. The hypothesised AI-vs-human SIGN of the diversity signals (AI-like prose: lower variance / entropy / delta-SD) is pinned in the test suite to catch the surprisal-detector family's shared silent-inversion failure mode. No new dependency, no version bump, no schema change. Spec: `specs/32-diveye-surprisal-diversity.md` (arXiv status PROVISIONAL on this checkout — the MAGE-OOD numbers are leads for the gated M2 experiment, not asserted facts).
