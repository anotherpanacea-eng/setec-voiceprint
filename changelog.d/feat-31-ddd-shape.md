### Added

**`dependency_distance_audit` — distribution-SHAPE descriptors of the per-link dependency-distance curve (spec 31; extends the existing spec-24 `voice_coherence` surface, no new surface).** The audit already emits the dependency-distance *distribution* (histogram + adjacent/long-range shares + the reused `mdd_mean`/`mdd_sd` from `variance_audit.mdd_stats`). This adds the **geometry of the curve itself** as a new additive `results["shape"]` block: the population variance/sd of the **pooled per-link distances**, the Fisher-Pearson moment skewness (g1) and excess kurtosis (g2), and the nearest-rank tail quantiles (`p50`/`p90`/`p99`/`max`). The shape `sd` is the within-POOL per-link SD — a **different quantity** from `mdd_sd`, which is the across-SENTENCE SD of per-sentence MDD means (two texts can match `mdd_mean`/`mdd_sd`/the shares and still differ in curve geometry; a regression test pins the distinctness). M1 = **pure stdlib** (`statistics`/`math` + arithmetic over the one `distances` list the audit already builds); **no numpy/scipy/torch**, no re-parse, and the shape math is unit-testable on a synthetic integer list with no parser at all. Clean-room of the descriptors from **The distribution of syntactic dependency distances** (Niu & Liu; [arXiv:2211.14620](https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.14620)), whose claim is that the *shape* of the DDD curve — right-skewed, heavy-tailed — is the stable cross-text object, not reducible to its mean.

Posture: **no verdict, no band** (the strictest no-verdict shape, like `originality_audit`) — values only, the envelope's claim-license refuses any AI/human, authorship, or writing-quality reading and states that skewness/excess_kurtosis are descriptive moments, **not** a complexity *score* and **not** an AI signal. Degenerate cases emit `null` (never a fabricated `0.0`): `skewness`/`excess_kurtosis` are `null` when `sd == 0` or `n_links < 3` (the standardized moments are undefined), while `variance`/`sd`/`quantiles` stay defined. A recursive `results`-walk posture guard forbids verdict/selection keys by **exact name** (so the legitimate, untouched `long_threshold` key is not mis-flagged), and an import-disjointness test keeps this descriptive surface out of every `voice_distance` / `surface_disagreement_resolver` held-out detector path (anti-Goodhart). R4-safe: the shape leaves carry no surprisal/probability token, so `validate_results_bounds` leaves them unchecked save the unconditional NaN/inf check, which the `null` handling avoids. Surface stays `voice_coherence` (no new task surface, no new `claim_license_surfaces/*.txt`); the capability fragment + per-id drop-in golden are updated in place (no `==N` count bump). The baseline-relative DDD distance and the parametric curve fit are flagged as separate future M2 specs.
