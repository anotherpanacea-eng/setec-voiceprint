### Added

**`rank_turbulence_audit` — interpretable per-word stylometric divergence (spec 23; `voice_coherence` surface).** An additive companion to `voice_distance`: where the Burrows Delta reports one aggregate distance, rank-turbulence divergence (RTD) reports *which words drive it*. Computes RTD ∈ [0,1] between a target and a `--baseline-dir`/`--manifest` over the **function-word distribution by default** (so it measures style, not topic), and lists the top per-word contributors over-ranked in the target vs the baseline. Clean-room reimplementation of RTD (Dodds et al. 2020, *Allotaxonometry and rank-turbulence divergence*, [arXiv:2002.09770](https://arxiv.org/abs/2002.09770); stylometric-Delta adaptation [arXiv:2604.19499](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19499)). Pure stdlib, deterministic, no model; tunable `--alpha` (default 1/3; smaller emphasizes rare words).

Posture: **no verdict** (`status: heuristic`). A high RTD is NOT "different author" and NOT "AI" — topic, genre, and length shift it; the `--all-words` mode is explicitly *topical, not stylometric* (off by default, warns). The claim-license refuses any authorship/AI inference; thresholds are operator-side / PROVISIONAL. Self-exclusion drops a baseline doc identical to the target path (mirrors `voice_distance`/`general_imposters`). Does **not** modify `voice_distance`. Reuses the existing `voice_coherence` surface (no new label). 13 stdlib tests.
