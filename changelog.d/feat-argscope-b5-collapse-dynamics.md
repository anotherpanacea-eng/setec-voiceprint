### Added

**`argument_decision_audit` (ArgScope) gains the two B5 collapse-dynamics signals — disappearing-guard + discounting-straw-men (heuristic, directional-no-anchor).** The surface now ships a new fifth bundle `B5_collapse_dynamics` carrying two arc-level (cross-paragraph) signals the per-paragraph {role, mode} schema cannot express: `disappearing_guard_flag` (a claim guarded/hedged early then treated as unguarded later — within-document hedging-drift) and `discounting_straw_men_flag` (engaging weak objections while leaving the strongest text-internal objection un-engaged — the paper's decoy-objection finding / dialectical-clarity OB5). They are derived from an **additive** judge-schema extension: each per-paragraph entry now also emits `guard_strength` (`strong`/`moderate`/`weak`/`none`/null) and a stable `claim_ref`; counterclaim/rebuttal paragraphs add an `objection_strength` (`strong`/`weak`/null); and one document-level `strongest_internal_objection_engaged` (bool|null) lands in `results.collapse_dynamics`. The legend instructs the judge to return null rather than fabricate, and the derivation returns None (never a fabricated False) when the evidence is absent. Both signals are `heuristic`, directional, with **NO numeric anchor** (the paper supports them only qualitatively and there is no measured discrimination) — pinned at the schema level and immune to register-baseline graduation, EXCLUDED from `aggregate_score` (`contribution=null`) and from the B1/B2 pre-flag, so the existing score and verdict band are numerically unchanged. They describe TEXTURE only and do not adjudicate fairness or soundness (that is banister / dialectical-clarity); the claim-license is updated accordingly. A pre-extension judge manifest still validates (missing new fields default to None; out-of-vocab values are nulled with a warning). Envelope change is additive (schema 1.0); the contract golden, `signals-glossary.md` counts, and the `argument_decision_audit` capability surface are updated. plugin.json is not bumped (versions bump at release).
