### Added

**`corpus_novelty_audit` + `skeleton_overlap_audit` — cross-document homogenization & originality (spec 28, M1; on the existing `set_level_diversity` surface).** Two *set-level* diagnostics no per-document or single-target surface can see. Both ride the shipped `set_level_diversity` surface — no new surface, no claim-license fragment, no count bump.

**`corpus_novelty_audit`** is the set-wide DJ-Search novelty *distribution*: where `originality_audit` scores one target against a pool, this scores a *corpus* against itself — for each document it runs DJ-Search reconstructibility against the rest of the corpus (leave-one-out) and reports the **distribution** of per-document `originality` (`{min/p25/median/p75/max/mean/sd}` + decile histogram), a per-document table (with the longest-matched-span `top_source`), and an ordered-pair mutual-reconstructibility census. It wraps the shipped `audit_originality` (clean-room DJ Search, *AI as Humanity's Salieri*, [arXiv:2410.04265](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.04265)) leave-one-out — it does **not** re-implement the matcher. **No single "corpus originality score" headline** — a lone scalar is a verdict in disguise; the distribution + table is the descriptive object.

**`skeleton_overlap_audit`** is the QUDsim cross-document discourse-skeleton axis ([arXiv:2504.09373](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.09373)), with a **model-free proxy** so M1 runs in CI: each discourse unit gets a content-word-free rhetorical-move signature (discourse-marker bucket × length-tercile × terminal-punctuation class), discretized to an ordered skeleton string per document; cross-document overlap is a normalized longest-common-subsequence ratio (stdlib `difflib`) yielding a descriptive `{mean/median/max}` pairwise overlap, a top-k pair table (with the shared aligned skeleton run), skeleton-similarity groups, and a readable per-document skeleton. **Topic-robust by construction** (content words excluded), so high overlap cannot be an artifact of a shared subject. The proxy is M1; the true LLM-parsed QUD lens is the gated M2 `--qud-lens model` (lazy-import, fail-loud `missing_dependency`, POC-gated).

Posture: **no verdict.** Low novelty / high skeleton-overlap is NOT "AI" — a tight prompt, a shared genre, a single source, or a house style all produce both legitimately; the claim-license refuses any AI/human, plagiarism, or selection determination, `--report-threshold` and `--mutual-share` only group/census for display (never a gate), and thresholds are operator-side / PROVISIONAL (`status: heuristic`). Both require ≥ `--min-docs` (default 3) documents and abstain below the floor (`bad_input`). Pure stdlib, deterministic, no model, no GPU; ships with 32 tests. M2's confident human/LLM band does not ship until a POC validates the QUD lens.
