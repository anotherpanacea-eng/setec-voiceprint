### Added

**`dependency_distance_audit` — the distribution of dependency distances (spec 24; `voice_coherence` surface).** A descriptive syntactic-*shape* profile: the linear span of each syntactic link (`d = |i − head.i|`). The *scalar* MDD (per-sentence mean + SD) already ships as `variance_audit.mdd_stats` (the `mdd_sd` signal) and is **reused verbatim** here — never re-implemented; the new, additive contribution is the **distribution** (the subject of [arXiv:2211.14620](https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.14620)): the dependency-distance histogram, the adjacent-link share (d=1), and the long-range tail (d ≥ threshold). The link set is pinned to match `mdd_stats` (punctuation kept, ROOT/self excluded), so the distribution is consistent with the scalar (`n_links == n_tokens − n_sentences`).

Parser-tier: reuses the shared spaCy pipeline (`variance_audit._NLP` / `en_core_web_sm`). With no parser the surface **abstains** (`available:false` / `missing_dependency`, the `general_imposters` whole-surface pattern) — there is no faithful parse-free dependency distance. Posture: **no verdict** (`status: heuristic`) — not authorship/AI, not a quality/readability score, and **NOT length-controlled** (MDD and the long-range share covary with sentence length and genre, so `mean_sentence_length` is co-reported so the confound is visible). 10 tests (parser-gated numeric pins `skipif(not HAS_SPACY)`; CI installs `en_core_web_sm`).
