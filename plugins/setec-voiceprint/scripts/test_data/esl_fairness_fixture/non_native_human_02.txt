My mother she always tell me that the food is the way how you show the love. In my family we are not saying "I love you" with the words very much, but my mother she is waking up early in the morning to prepare the breakfast for everybody, and this is her way to say it. When I am leaving to study abroad, she is crying but she does not say much, only she pack for me a big box of the dried fruits and the spices so I can make the taste of home.

The first months I am trying to cook her recipes but it is never the same. The vegetables here they taste different, more watery, and I cannot find the correct spices in the small shop near my house. I am calling her on the phone and she is explaining me step by step, but she never use the measures, she only say "you put until it feel correct." This is very difficult for me because I want the exact numbers.

One day my flatmate, she is from another country also, and she is missing her home food too. So we decide we cook together, each one teaching the other. The kitchen become very messy and we are making many mistakes, but we laugh so much. The food is not perfect like our mothers make, but it have something else inside it, maybe the friendship.

Now when I cook, I am not following the recipe so strict. I put the spices until it feel correct, like my mother say. Finally I am understanding what she mean all this time.
