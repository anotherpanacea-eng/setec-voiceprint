I want to write about my city because I love it very much. My city is not big but it have many old buildings and a river that go through the middle. In the summer the people they sit near the river in the evening and they eat ice cream and talk. The children they play football in the small park and the old mens they play the chess on the stone tables.

The best time in my city is the festival in the autumn. All the streets they are decorated with the lights and the flags. My grandmother she tell me that this festival is more than two hundred years old. Every family they cook the special bread and they give it to the neighbours. When I am small I am thinking everybody in the world have this festival but now I know it is only my city.

The food in my city is very simple but very delicious. We eat much bread and cheese and the soup with the beans. My favourite is the sweet cake that my aunt make only for the special days. She put inside the nuts and the honey and it is so good that I eat too much and after my stomach is paining.

I am living now in another country for the study and I am missing my city every day. When I am sad I am looking the photos and I am remember the smell of the bread and the sound of the river. One day I will go back and I will bring my friends to show them this beautiful place.
