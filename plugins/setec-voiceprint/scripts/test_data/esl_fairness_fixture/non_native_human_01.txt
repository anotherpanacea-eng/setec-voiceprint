When I was come to this country first time, everything is very different for me. The winter is very cold, more cold than my home city, and I do not have the correct coat. My first week I am walking to the university every day and my ears are paining because of the wind. A woman in the bus, she see me shivering and she give me her extra scarf. I am very surprised because in my culture also we help strangers but here I do not expect it.

The language is the most difficult thing. I am studying English since I am a child but the people here they speak very fast and they use many words I never learn in the textbook. Sometimes I am nodding my head and smiling but I do not understand anything. This is making me feel stupid even though in my own language I am a person who talk a lot and make jokes.

Slowly slowly it is becoming better. I make one friend in my chemistry class, his name is Daniel, and he is correcting my mistakes but in a kind way, not laughing. He teach me the slang words and I teach him to cook the food from my country. We are laughing a lot when he is trying to eat the spicy things.

Now after two years I am feeling this place is like a second home for me. I still make many grammar mistakes when I am writing, I know this, but I am not so afraid anymore to speak and to be wrong.
