The machinery of the family operates with predictable failure modes. Every gear in the mechanism catches against the others. The engine of resentment turns through the long winter without rest.

The mechanics of family arrangement reveal themselves only when the system breaks down. A loose bolt in the machinery of obligation makes the whole apparatus shake on its mountings. The engine of holiday tradition runs on a fuel the family has never managed to refine.

The simple machinery of the household keeps the larger machinery of the family running. Each mechanism depends on the one beside it; no single engine carries the weight alone.
