Widget Tariffs in Focus

This short In Focus product summarizes recent changes to widget tariff schedules. It is a two-page snapshot rather than a developed analysis, and it states the current rates, the effective dates, and the agencies responsible for administration. It does not advance a recommendation or weigh competing options, and so it falls below the argument-depth floor this acquisition applies to the policy-brief baseline.
